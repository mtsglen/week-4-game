Puppy Collector!

You will be given a randomly generated number at the beginning of the game.


There are four puppies below. By clicking on a puppy you will add a specific amount of points to your overall score.


You win the game by matching your total score to a random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.


The value of each puppy is hidden from you until you click on it.


Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each puppy.
